About Me

My name is Mariana Pereira and I am a Portuguese Architect currently studying to become a Full Stack Web Developer. I was born in Potugal on the 1st of December 1994 and lived there until April 2018 when I moved to Stockholm, Sweden.
I am a very social person and love to spend time outdoors. Reading is one of my big passions and I also greatly enjoy a night out in the movies or the theather. However, the thing I love doing the most is spending time with my friends or getting to know new people.
Travelling is another of my passions and, even though I still have not had the opportunity to travel a lot, discovering new places, cultures and architecture is a huge part of my dreams for the future.
In terms of Architecture the fields that interest me the most are environmental sustainability, old buildings rehabilitation and convertion, plus housing and hotel architecture.
I decided to study Web Development since it is a field that has always drawn my interest. Even though I am still a beginner in this field I am enjoying every second of it and I feel that my architectural background will be a benefit for my future in Web Development since it gives me a great sense of design and creative skills.
I consider myself to be an extremelly determined and hard-working person. I work well as a team and, most of all, I love to learn new things, as well as to face new challenges.
